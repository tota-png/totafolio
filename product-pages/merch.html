Merch Design

Throughout my undergraduate career, I have had the pleasure to work with various clubs to design new merch items. This page showcases some of the real works and conceptual products designed for the Department of Chemistry, the Department of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology, and more.

While brainstorming potential merch items for the upcoming school year, the Chemistry Club considered selling totes to fund the club. Ultimately, none were made, but this was one of the designs I created. As a chapter of the American Chemical Society, we pride ourselves in promoting the principles of Green Chemistry, all of which are listed in this handy tote. Designed June 2024.

The "spaghettification" shirt was made to celebrate the end of the 2023-2023 school year. For some unknown reason, all club shirts thus far have been space themed, and this one was no exception. On the right are the Spring 2023 Short Form performers donning the illustrated shirt.
